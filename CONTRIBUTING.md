# Contributing to MVAP

Thank you for your interest in contributing to the **MVAP** (Model of Volitional Adaptivity of the Psyche) project!

This is an open research project dedicated to developing a realization-independent theory of individual adaptivity, consciousness, and strong AI. We welcome contributions that help improve the quality, clarity, and accessibility of the materials.

## How Can You Contribute?

We accept the following types of contributions:

- **Bug reports and suggestions** — typos, inaccuracies, unclear formulations
- **Improvements to documentation** — editing README, glossary, or book chapters
- **Translations** — into other languages (especially important)
- **New materials** — additional articles, diagrams, explanations, or examples
- **Code contributions** — improvements to the Beast prototype or new prototypes
- **Visualizations** — diagrams, illustrations, mind maps of the Egostat architecture
- **Feedback** — critical and constructive comments from the perspective of neuroscience, AI, philosophy, or cognitive science
